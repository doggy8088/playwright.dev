[Page.clock](https://ollama.com/library)() 覆蓋了與時間相關的原生全域類別和函式，允許手動控制它們:   - `Date`   - `setTimeout`   - `clearTimeout`   - `setInterval`   - `clearInterval`   - `requestAnimationFrame`   - `cancelAnimationFrame`   - `requestIdleCallback`   - `cancelIdleCallback`   - `performance`   - `Event.timeStamp`
