:::note
[Page.Clock](/api/class-page.mdx#page-clock) 覆蓋了與時間相關的內建全域類別和函式，使它們可以手動控制:   - `Date`   - `setTimeout`   - `clearTimeout`   - `setInterval`   - `clearInterval`   - `requestAnimationFrame`   - `cancelAnimationFrame`   - `requestIdleCallback`   - `cancelIdleCallback`   - `performance`   - `Event.timeStamp`
:::
