- [Expect(Locator).ToHaveAccessibleNameAsync()](https://api/class-locatorassertions.mdx#locator-assertions-to-have-accessible-name) 檢查元素是否具有指定的可存取名稱:

  ```csharp
  var locator = Page.GetByRole(AriaRole.Button);
  await Expect(locator).ToHaveAccessibleNameAsync("Submit");
  ```

  ```csharp
  var locator = Page.GetByRole(AriaRole.Button);
  await Expect(locator).ToHaveAccessibleDescriptionAsync("Upload a photo");
  ```

- [Expect(Locator).ToHaveRoleAsync()](https://api/class-locatorassertions.mdx#locator-assertions-to-have-role) 檢查元素是否具有指定的 ARIA 角色:

  ```csharp
  var locator = Page.GetByTestId("save-button");
  ```
